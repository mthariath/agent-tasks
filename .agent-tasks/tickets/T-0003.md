## Context

The TUI now has planning intelligence, but choosing the next ticket should feel obviously better than scanning raw columns.

## Outcome

The planning experience clearly surfaces ready work, parallelizable groups, and blocker leverage without overwhelming the main shell.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Planning view makes ready-now work immediately obvious.
- Parallelizable waves and blocker-heavy tickets are easy to scan.
- Keyboard flow between board, people, and plan views feels coherent.
- The shell makes it easy to decide what to pick next.

## Notes

Do not turn this into a scheduler. Keep it advisory and fast.
