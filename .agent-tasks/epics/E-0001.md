## Goal

Make the TUI feel solid enough for daily use, especially around responsiveness, live updates, and choosing the next ready work.

## Scope

- Improve responsive board behavior so columns remain usable at common terminal widths.
- Verify and harden realtime refresh behavior and selection persistence.
- Tighten planning/discovery surfaces so it is easier to pick ready and parallelizable work.

## Notes

The goal is practical usability, not decorative polish.
